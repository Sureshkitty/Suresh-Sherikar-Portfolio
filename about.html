About Me

Hi! I'm Suresh Sherikar, a B.Tech student in Mechanical Engineering with a strong passion for IT, software development, and emerging technologies. I specialize in front-end development, creating engaging user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

In addition to my engineering studies, I have completed a Diploma in Computer Applications (DCA), which strengthened my foundational skills in software tools, programming, and IT systems. I also completed a course from The Open University (UK), broadening my global perspective and technical knowledge.

I bring 1 year of professional experience as a Computer Operator at Balaji DTP, where I worked on:

  • Report creation in MS Word
  • Presentations in PowerPoint
  • Excel data listing, cleaning, and analysis

Beyond web development, I have a growing interest in data analysis. I use tools like Excel, SQL, and Python to extract insights, create visualizations, and support smart decision-making. I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful stories.

I’m also passionate about embedded systems and automation. With hands-on experience using Arduino, I design smart solutions integrating sensors and microcontrollers for applications like home automation and environmental monitoring.

I'm continuously learning and exploring new technologies to stay current with industry trends. My goal is to blend creativity, analytical thinking, and technical skills to develop real-world solutions that create impact.
